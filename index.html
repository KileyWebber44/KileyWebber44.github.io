ABOUT ME

My name is Kiley Webber, and I am a university student pursuing Human Factors and Engineering Psychology! I am working to combine a passion for technology, helping others, and the human mind with my studies and future career. I have research skills such as survey creation and data analysis and am competent with SPSS for statistical analysis. I also have participated Humanities research, presenting a paper on Alexander Hamilton's infamous Reynolds Affair at the National Conference of Undergraduate Research in 2019.

Aside from psychology and other academic work I am an aspiring artist,primarily doing character design, fantasy art, and graphics. I am also trained in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.
